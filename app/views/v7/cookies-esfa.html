Cookies on Education and Skills Funding Agency (ESFA) service pages

Cookies are files saved on your phone, tablet or computer when you visit a website.

We use cookies to store information about how you use ESFA services.

Cookie settings

We use 2 types of cookies. You can choose whether or not to allow Google Analytics cookies:

Cookies that measures website use

We use Google Analytics to measure how you use the website so we can improve it based on user needs. We do not allow Google to use or share the data about how you use this site.

Google Analytics sets cookies that store anonymised information about:

  • how you got to our sites
  • the pages you visit on ESFA sites
  • how long you spend on each ESFA page
  • what you click on while you’re visiting our sites.

Strictly necessary cookies

These essential cookies make our services work in your browser.

They always need to be on.

Find out more about ESFA's cookies

Government services

Different government services are run by different departments. These services may set additional cookies and, if so, will have their own cookie policy and banner linking to it.
